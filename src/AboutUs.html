We are a team of five student of Powercoders Milan bootcamp batch of 2021.We are working to develop a website called TestPot as part of our project of bootcamp.


Right now the world is in an emergency health situation in which the covid-19 raveged the whole world by disrupting people social and economic activies.


The burdern to the economy and loss of life become enournmous challenge to individual and govenments through out the world.


Out of Powercoders bootcamp students, born the idea of TestPot website which help the user to find the nearest covid-19 test center.


The website will be useful to help people with information as well as locating the test centers.


we are very grateful to get the opportunity to train as a web developer and would like to give back by putting this website in to work.
